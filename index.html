About

Fin is an Analyst in Ampol's Future Energy - Commercial Finance team, focused on energy transition and decarbonisation, supporting the company's goal of net zero by 2040.

Previously a Manager in EY's Transaction Diligence practice across Sydney and New York, Fin has executed buy-side and sell-side financial due diligence across M&A transactions for private equity and corporate clients globally - spanning adjusted EBITDA analysis, working capital, net debt, and SPA/PPA review.

CA-accredited (Chartered Accountants ANZ). Bachelor of Accounting Co-op (Distinction) from UTS Sydney. Motivated by challenge professionally and personally, including humanitarian projects in Cambodia and competitive sport.
